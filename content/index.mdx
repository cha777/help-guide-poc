# Introduction

**Pro12** is designed to help you achieve your trading and investment goals with **All the best tools in one platform**.

![Pro12](/home.png)

## Features

- Excellent performance & speed.
- Realtime market data, news, announcements & analytical tools
- Full Financial Statements & Financial Ratios.
- Pre-built dashboards for easy & quick navigation with the ability to build custom dashboards & workspaces.
- Advanced new professional charting with increased line studies, indicators, technical strategies & a new comprehensive custom indicator formula builder.
- Watchlist filtering based on custom formulas with realtime alerts.
- Sector Cash Flow Tracker.
- Numerical Analyzer & Technical Scanner.
- Enhanced Corporate Events functionality.
- Stock effect on TASI and Sector Indices.
- Big Trades Widget.
